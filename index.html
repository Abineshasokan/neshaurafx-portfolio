05 / The Workflow

Simple Process.
Professional Results.

How we collaborate from the raw source files to a polished, high-performing output.

01

Send Your Footage

Share your raw recording links (Drive, Dropbox, Frame.io) along with your styling references, platform specifications, and core content goals.

02

Creative Planning

We analyze your footage structure and construct a tailored engagement roadmap, planning hooks, text placements, sound patterns, and narrative flow.

03

Editing & Enhancement

The magic happens. Footage is assembled, paced, color graded, and injected with custom transitions, typography, SFX, and graphics.

04

Review & Revisions

Review the draft on a collaborative frame-sharing app, provide feedback, and we polish up the visual details until the clip is absolutely perfect.

05

Final Delivery

Get your export files in platform-native optimized codecs and sizes, complete with caption scripts and title cues, ready to publish and capture views.
